Primary

Ensure that the wordmark has a sufficient amount of clear space around it so that it is visible and distinct from other graphic elements. Our icon (what we like to call the "checkmark") can be used as a supplementary identifying mark or decorative element. Typeface

Avenir Next is our primary typeface and should be used whenever possible. The bold weight is used in our logo and may also be used in the site headings. The demi bold weight is also used in the logo and may be used in site buttons. Lastly, the regular weight may be used in body text, captions, and forms throughout the site.

Color Scheme

Our color palette consists of three primary colors (navy, red, muted white) and two secondary colors (muted red, muted navy). The primary colors should be strictly maintained throughout the site's design and must remain solid and contain no gradients or changes to transparency (unless it's being used on an image). The secondary colors may only be utilized in buttons when they're in the hover state. Our muted white (#F8F8F8) must be used in the body text and headings when the background is Navy (#052640) or Red (#F04E36). If the background is muted white then either Navy or Red (sparingly) may be utilized for the body text and headings.

UI Elements

These elements may be used sparingly throughout the site and must keep the font and colors consistent. There are four different button styles that may be used depending on the background where it is utilized. Any social media icons that are needed in the site should come from the FontAwesome Webkit included in this HTML document. Social media icons must use the square version and may only be integrated within the header, footer, and/or contact page. Social media icons may only be changed to any of the three primary colors. Also remember to link each icon to its respective social media page.
