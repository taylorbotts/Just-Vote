About Us

Our History

Just Vote was created by five UCF students who thought our community was missing something. None of us felt encouraged to get out and vote and truly show our voice and as a collective, we thought we needed to encourage fellow students ourselves. With this one idea, Just Vote was created by UCF students for UCF students just in time for the 2020 election.

Our idea is simple and truly is to “just vote”. We want to make things easy and don’t want to sway anyone’s political opinion, all we want is to empower young people to take on their duty and get out and vote. At Just Vote, we believe our voice can be heard through social media and social media alone, not campaigning on campus and pushing people away. With this, we encourage you to follow us, that way you can easily be reminded of voting deadlines, candidates and all things Just Vote!

2020 is such a major election year and now more than ever, young adults opinions are valued and need to be heard. Taking the steps to vote is easy and takes very little time. Our site offers quick links to check your registration, voter locations and dates.

Still not sure who you want to vote for? Take a look at our candidates page to get an idea who is running for president. We also suggest taking the “Who I Side With” quiz which can be found here.

Let’s charge on to the voting polls!
